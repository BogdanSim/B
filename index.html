A few words about me

In my everyday life I am a father, a passionate self-taught person in front-end development field, skilled in React, JavaScript, HTML, SASS and more, with ambition and desire of daily development and improvement, designer and a part-time student.

Things I'm familiar with:

  • - Translating designs UX wireframes and mockups into responsive, interactive features, using HTML/CSS and JavaScript for small - medium sized businesses
  • - Working with css preprocessors sass
  • - Development of adaptive design with the Bootstrap 5 framework
  • - Using React libraries
  • - Administrating websites & online shops
  • - Designing logos & labels, ability to use Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDraw, Canva.
